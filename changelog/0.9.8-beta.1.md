## Onboarding is now just permissions

The old first-run tour (workflow choice, checklists, acknowledgments) is gone.
On first launch Videorc now shows one thing: the three macOS permissions it can
use — Screen Recording, Camera, and Microphone — each with a live status chip
and a button that asks macOS right there. Camera and microphone prompt in
place; Screen Recording jumps you to the exact System Settings pane and the
chip flips green the moment you grant it.

Nothing is required: grant what you need and continue. And if your Mac already
has the grants — including after a reinstall — onboarding never appears at all.
You can reopen it any time from Settings → System access → **Set up
permissions**.

## 4K recording, free

Recording at 4K is no longer a Premium feature — every tier can record at full
4K. Streaming at 4K remains Premium.

## Fixes

- The **Circle** camera bubble now stays perfectly round in the native
  preview. It could render as a slight oval when the preview's proportions
  drifted from the output's; the preview now matches the recording exactly.
